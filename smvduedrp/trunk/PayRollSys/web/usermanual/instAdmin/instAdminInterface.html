PayRoll System

    The Payroll system is software which organizes all the task of employee payment and the filing of employee taxes. These tasks can include keeping tracking of hours, calculating wages, withholding taxes and deductions, printing and delivering checks and paying employment taxes to the government. Enter your email ID and password in given window and click on login. To login as the institute admin click on the link click here given.

Financial Years

    Configure existing financial years.Click on Add New and fill the details for creating a new session and click on save.Now to activate a session check the box in the current session and click on update.

Department

    Configure department details.To add a single department click on Add New. Fill the details required and click on save as shown below.
    To add more than one departments click on Upload Department List. Instruction for upload a csv file.
    1. Open LibreOffice Calc in ubuntu and Excel in windows.
    2. The file should contain three field i.e Department Code, Department Name and Department Nick Name
    Example:
    Department Code = CS
    Department Name =Computer Science Engineering
    Department Nick Name = CSE
    Click on add button to add the csv file and then click on update to upload the file.

Pay Scales

    Instruction for upload a csv file.
    1. Open LibreOffice Calc in ubuntu and Excel in windows.
    2. The file should contain four field i.e
    Pay Band Name,Max Amount,Min Amount and Academic Grade Pay/ Grade Pay.
    Example:
    Pay Band Name = PB-2
    Max Amount = 33900
    Min Amount =9300
    Grade Pay = 3800
    3. Save as csv format.
    Upload the csv file and click on Update.

    Example:

Salary Formula

    Instruction for Add and Update Salary Formula
    Click on the link which is given in Action column
    After click there is a pop up panel on your screen which contain Salary Head and empty area given to insert the formula for the Head.
    e.g. : if central government pay 119% DA (Dearness Allowance) then formula is (BP+GP)*1.19 (here BP and GP is the Short Name
    of the Basic Pay and Grade Pay). use Short Name of the Salary Head for creating formula.
    Note: exactly you follow the above example for creating the formula for the Salary Head
