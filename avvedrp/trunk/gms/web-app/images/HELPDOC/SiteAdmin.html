Registration

Site Admin creation

As a first step towards registering the institution, a site administrator person’s profile is created.

  • The details saved for this profile are – Full name, password, email and the unique institution code.
  • This site admin is the key representative of the institution and uses the GMS on behalf of the institution.
  • So, he has all the related access/privileges such as – adding employees, projects, account heads etc, assigning roles & privileges to other users and first and foremost – completing the  record of institution details.

Profile Activation

  • After creation of site admin a confirmation email with the activation link and login details will be sent to the email address entered during registration.  
  • Account will get activated by clicking the activation link provided in the email.

 

Updating Institution details

  • After activation, the institution can login to their account, and update the institution details.
  • The following institution details are saved in record, mandatorily-- name, institution code, address, phone and email.
  • The details may be edited /updated any number of times later on.

Creating Departments for the Institution

The institution can create departments within (if required).

  • The mandatory details saved are: Department name and code.
  • The user may set a department as active/inactive as appropriate in case of future changes in the department structure. Thus system can retain all the past data for a currently inactive department but not display or involve it in the current activities & reports, once the “Inactive” selection is made.
  • The details may be edited /updated any number of times later on.
