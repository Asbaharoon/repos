Proposal Management

For each institution's privileged user, this section lists out the (request-for-proposal) notifications published by all the other institutions in the GMS for various projects.

On this page, the interested institution users (prospective grantees) can:

  • View the notification title, code, notification date, grantor institution’s name, last date for submission of proposal etc.
  • View the notification details
  • Upload their proposal thereby setting a status “Submitted” and then
  • Apply for the grant scheme detailed by the notification

Clicks on Projects tab, to expand the menu and then click Proposal Management.

In the new page, the notifications released by the other institutions in GMS netowrk are displayed.

Please note that the notifications listed here are intended for private view only (the Public? flag is set to "N"at the time of creating notification) . Only GMS users in the network can view the notifications in this internal Notification List, submit proposals and apply.
