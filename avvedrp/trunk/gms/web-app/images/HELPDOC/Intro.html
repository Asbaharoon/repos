Multisite Grant Management System

 

MGMS, a module of Sakshat ERP has been designed to meet the unique needs within higher education and research institutes, including the specific needs of individual departments, centers, schools, colleges, and universities who receives Grants from Government / Non-Governmental Organizations and fruitfully utilizes to complete a project for which the grant is allocated.

It is an enterprise-wide system specifically designed for the needs of Funding and Grant making organizations. It controls all aspects of the funding process – from initial request, through assessment and recommendations, to authorization, payment and subsequent monitoring of outcomes.

MGMS also helps with accountability to MHRD, Govt. of India and provides additional online information regarding funds appropriated, allocated; the number of projects awarded; with projects begun and projects completed; and the number of jobs created or sustained by funds.

Designed and developed by Amrita Vishwa Vidya Peetham, MGMS is highly configurable and this flexibility supports the modern funding organization’s need to adapt and change.

Features of MGMS

  • Interactive Project Dashboard.
  • Multi-level user management
  • MIS Reports and Statistical Reports
  • Keeps track of the Project Details such as Project activities, resources, duration and Project Status.
  • Head of Accounts management at various levels.
  • Tracking grant period details on various projects.
  • Fund Allocation for various Projects.
  • Outsourcing of Sub Projects as well as management of Subprojects within the Organization.
  • Grant Allocation Management.
  • Expenses entry for various projects on different heads of accounts.
  • Extensive and detailed reports based on multi criteria.
