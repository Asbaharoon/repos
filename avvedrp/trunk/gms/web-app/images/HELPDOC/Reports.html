Reports

Multiple types of reports are available in the system related to specific projects details as well as to institution wide data. In addition, the utilization reports submitted by sub projects are also available here.

Project Reports:

  1. Head Wise Expenditure

This report gives the amount allocated (budgeted amount) to and the total expenses incurred by each of the account heads to which the project is associated.

  1. Institution wise Consolidated Expenditure

This report gives the fund balance information for all its subprojects that have been assigned to various grantee institutions.

  1. Fund Utilization

This report gives the details of the actual cash flow out of the user’s institution, by way of account head expenses, fund transferred to grantee institutions etc .

  1. Grand Summary Report

This report lists out all the sub projects assigned by it to other institutions (grantees) and the associated details –

    • grantee name & project code,
    • amount allocated to the grantee,
    • funds received by grantee,
    • balance to be sent to the grantee,
    • funds utilized by the grantee for the project and
    • any outstanding amount (%)
  1. Grant Agency Reports

For the specified grantor institution, this report displays:

  • all its grantee institutions
  • the number of sub-projects assigned to each of these grantee institutions
  • the total funds allocated (sanctioned) to each of these grantee institutions
  • the total funds already transferred to each of these grantee institutions
  • the balance to be transferred to each of these grantee institutions
  • the balance to be transferred as a percentage of the total fund allocated
  1. Institution Reports

This report lists out all the funds that have been sanctioned for the user’s institution (grantee) to receive from various grant agencies.

  1. Project Associate Reports

This report lists out the institution’s own projects with associated details such as project head, manager, allocated amount (self fund), received amount, balance expected, funds transferred to grantees, own account head expenses, and balance fund in hand.

Project Status Reports:

  1. Grant Allocation Status Report

 

This report gives the list of grantee institutions currently receiving grants from the user’s institution and their corresponding - grant allocation amount, status of the grant allocation, date of tracking status and the remarks for that tracking instance. User may generate lists for each status.

  1. Project Status Report

 

This report lists out all the instances of tracking done for the selected project. Each row gives the date of tracking, the associated project status, percentage of completion of project and any remarks

Grant Agency Reports:
This set of reports give information about the pre-grant activities concerning the projects to be allocated by the user’s institution

  1. Notification Report

This report gives notification details for every project to be allocated.

  1. Received Proposals Report

This report gives the list of projects for which proposals have been received

  1. Pending Proposals Report

As the name suggests this report lists out the names of the parties (grantees) from whom proposals are pending for different projects

  1. List of grantees

As the name suggests, this report gives details (name, code, email, phone no: etc) of the current grantees of the institution – the institutions to which some projects have already been assigned.

 

Utilization Certificate
This document certifies to the total amount utilized towards the project, from the funds received and the balance amount remaining.

Statement of Accounts
The report gives the cash flow in and out of the institution, for a project since its initiation till current date of generation of statement of accounts.

This includes details such as total grant allocated, amount received till now, total expenditure (account head expenses + funds transferred to other institutes as subproject allocations) and finally closing balance
