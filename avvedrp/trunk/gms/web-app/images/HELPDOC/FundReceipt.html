Fund Receipt

This is a record of acknowledgement of amount received by a grantee from the grantor. The fund transferred by the grantor and received by the grantee maintains a 1- to-1 relationship.

  • A receipt may be recorded by:
    • An account head of a grantee institution which received an amount (fund transfer) against the sub-allocation made to it by the grantor institution for a project.
        For a project selected from the Project List, click Expenses in the top row menu, and then sub-menu Fund Receipt.
        • The details associated with a grant receipt are:
            • Amount
            • Date of receipt
            • Funds received order no:
            • Grant allocation against which fund was transferred
            • Fund transferred against which grant was received
            • Receiving account head name
            • Mode of payment and related details (Cash/DD/cheque details, bank details etc)

         

        • Each grant receipt may be tracked by Fund received Order no.
        • The information maybe updated later. But please note that the amount remains the same as that was transferred and is not editable.
