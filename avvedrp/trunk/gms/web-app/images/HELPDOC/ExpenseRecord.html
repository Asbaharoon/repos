Expense Entry

As the name suggests, this functionality enables the recording of expenses by the various account /sub account heads of the institution. The expenses are categorized into account heads, for e.g., manpower, material purchase, finance etc
These are recorded in this section against the specific grant allocation which is expected to pay for the expenses.

The details saved for each expense entry include: the amount, date of expense, grant allocation against which the expense may be put, associated account head name, mode of payment, payment details (DD/Cheque, bank details etc) and the user’s description of the expense item.

  • Each expense entry can be tracked through:
    • Option to view/edit Date wise expense list for user provided criteria of expense period
    • Option to view account head wise expense summary.
