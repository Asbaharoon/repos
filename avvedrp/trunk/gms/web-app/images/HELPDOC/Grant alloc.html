Allocation of Grants to various Account heads

Account Heads are created for convenient management of received funds and expenses incurred in a project. At present, 2-levels of account heads can be created – account heads and sub account heads.

  • The institution can divide the allocation for a project to different account heads and sub-account heads.
  • This will be done against a grant period and displayed as a pie chart in the GMS application
  • For each allocation to a head, the details saved are – grant period, account head name, sub-account head name, the amount and any description by the user.
  • Editing/updating is possible any number of times

For a specific project selected from the Project List, click Allocation on the top row menu and then sub menu - Disburse Fund against Against Account heads.

Initially, only the grants allocated to the project till now are listed out, along with date of allocation and grantor name, but each line item having a Break up link.

Click Break up for a particular allocation and the Disburse Fund page opens up.
