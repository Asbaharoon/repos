A

Account Head
It a record of debit and credit entries to cover transactions involving a particular item or concern associates with the project. For e.g. Travel expense, personnel, office supplies etc.

Allocation
It is the official assignment of financial resources in an economic way. In GMS, there are 2 scenarios of allocation:

  • The grantor allocating a bulk amount for a project to the grantee.
  • The allocation of amount amongst the various expense and related account heads associated with the project, within the organization (grantor's or grantee's).

Approval Authority
It is a review panel composed of experts who may or may not directly belong to the granting agencies and who review the grant applications to select eligible applications and reject others. They make funding recommendations to the granting agency. An approval authority may have one or more members, each of whom reads many proposals during the review process, to collectively arrive at a shortlist and thereafter the final approved proposal.

In the following scenarios in GMS, approval authorities have a role:

  • Internal evaluation of pre-proposals
  • Internal evaluation of full proposals
  • External granting agency evaluation of submitted proposals

Assign proposal to Approval Auhority
Each pre-proposal and subsequent full proposal that is submitted by the staff in the institution is assigned to teams of review members - approval authorities, to be evaluated, in a specific order.The pre-proposal details get populated for each member of the approval authorities to whom the specific pre-proposal was assigned. Each evaluation level gets to review only after the previous level has completed.

Attachment Type
As the name suggests, this attribute helps the user mark a certain attached document as of a certain type (user defined), for future reference. For e.g. quarterly report can be an attachment type. So also, software license fee receipt.

 

B

Balance Amount
(As displayed in Sub project allotment screen) This is the amount remaining with the grantee from the original allocation after allocating further to sub projects. .

 

 

C

Co-PI
Co-PI is an individual involved with the PI in the scientific development or execution of a project. Currently in GMS, the co-investigator is assumed to be from the same institute as that doing the associated project. A co-investigator is considered “key personnel.”

Current Balance
(As displayed in Project Info screen) - this is the actual fund remaining with the grantee after utilization by self and transferring to sub projects. .

CV - Curriculum Vitae
A CV is a full account of a person's academic credentials and accomplishments. Granting agencies sometimes require a "shortened CV" of the proposed PI that lists only selected pieces of scholarly history.

 

 

D

Department
Department is a specialized division in the institution formed to organize people, reporting relationships and are usually organized by functions and domains such as human resources, marketing, administration, and sales.In educational institutions, there are academic groups and examples are Department of Computer Science, Department of Biotechnology etc.

Department Code
In MGMS, Department code is intended to uniquely identify each department of the registered institution. Currently the application would accept an arbitrary code which is familar to the user and display the code only in all relevant screens. Ideally, any coding system followed in the records of the particular institution can be implemented here.

Document Type
In MGMS, documents are uploaded in many modules for various purposes. To identify these attachments, categories have been used in the application - such as proposal, project, invoice etc called document types. Attachment types can be defined within these document types as per user's choice.
