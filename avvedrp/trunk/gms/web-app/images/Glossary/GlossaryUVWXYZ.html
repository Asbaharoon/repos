U

Unsolicited proposal
An unsolicited proposal is submitted on the initiative of the submitter to a sponsoring agency and not in response to any formal or informal request.

User
User is an individual who has a unique identity across an enterprise using the MGMS. The user is assigned an enterprise role that determines the user's access privileges in the MGMS.

Utilization
Utilization is the sum total of all expenses incurred towards a project. In MGMS currently, utilization fo the finances is reported on by grantees to their grantors. .

Utilization report
A utilization report provides data on where and how resources are being used. When a grantee submits the report to its grantor for a project, the utilization includes those expenses incurred by the grantee's sub grantees (sub projects) as well.

Withdrawal
When a grantor decides to stop funding a specific project, it withdraws the grant from it. This means that the grantor will no longer support the particular project in any manner.
