E

Eligibility Criteria
These are specific criteria by which proposals are judged in the first round of evaluation. These are objective in nature and used for the elimination process rather than ranking. The criteria are intimated to the applicants and the submitted proposals are expected to address them.

Employee ID
Unique identifier assigned to an employee by the employing institution.

Employee Designation
This is the official job title of the employee in the particular institution as assigned by the employer. .

Expense
It is the outflow of money from the institution to an external entity as payment for a product or service rendered to it, for a particular project.

F

Full Proposal
Full proposal which is submitted to an internal review board or approval authority is expected to contain all details concerning the project proposal which the applicant hopes to submit to the external funding agency/institution, if approved internally.

Fund
Fund denotes the monetary value of amounts sanctioned, allocated, transferred, received, expended/utilized etc by the various institutions during the execution of a project as per any specific grant.

Fund Awaiting
This is the amount remaining from the total allocation, to be received from the grantor by the grantee. It is calculated as the difference of amount allocated and amount received.

Fund Receipt
It is the acknowledgement recorded by a grantee in the MGMS, of having received the amount transferred to it by its grantor for a specific project. To be included in the grantee's accounts, all fund transferred to it must be received.

Fund Receipt No:
This is the reference number or unique identifier to be recorded for each fund receipt acknowledgement. Ideally the exact reference used in the official records may be entered in this field.

Fund Transfer
This is the process of transferring an amount by a grantor to a grantee as per a certain grant allocation. It is not required that the entire allocated amount be transferred in a single transaction. Multiple fund transfers can happen to cover a single allocation..

Fund Transfer No:
This is the reference number or unique identifier to be recorded for each fund transfer. Ideally the exact reference used in the official records may be entered in this field.

 

G

Grant
A grant is an actual award of monetary/financial assistance - funds given by an agency - private or public to an eligible entity to carry out an approved project or activity.

Grant Agency - Grantor
The agency, institution or individual which is formally awarding the grant. Generally there is no substantial programmatic involvement by the granting agency in the activities.

Grantee
The agency, institution or individual to whom a grant is formally awarded. Grants are generally made to universities, school districts or other non-profit organizations, and only rarely to individuals. The grantee is legally responsible and accountable to the grantor for the performance and financial aspects of the grant-supported project.
