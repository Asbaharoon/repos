R

Reviewer
A reviewer is an individual who evaluates the submitted proposals at various stages in the MGMS pre grant process, to approve and rank eligible ones and elmininate the others. Internal reviewers evaluate pre-proposals and full proposals. Reviewers are assigned in panels called approval authorities and are expected to use their relevant domain expertise while evaluating the proposals.

Role
A named group of related privileges. The site administrator can grant a role to a specific group of users thereby assigning them the same set of privileges and permissions.

 

S

Salary Component
The various constituents that make up the total salary  for an employee in an institution. The components may include the basic salary, medical allowance, travel allowance, conveyance, provident fund etc

Sanction Order No:
This is a unique identfier created by the sponsoring agency/grantor and assigned to a specific grant amount agreement - and is used as a reference thereafter.

Statement of Account
It lists out all the debits and credits over the relevant time period, for a given project being executed at a specific institution. In MGMS, this includes funds received from grantor, funds utilized and funds transferred to sub grantees.

Site Admin
By definition, the site administrator has total access and all permissions for total control of the activities happening for his institution in the MGMS.

Solicited proposal
Solicited proposals are written in response to published requirements.

Sub account head
An account within an account. For example, the Travel expense account may have sub accounts - air fare, domestic air, local train etc.

Sub grantee
It is an institution or person that is awarded the subproject and is accountable to the grantee for the utilisation of the resources.

Sub project
It is a collaborative arrangement in support of a project in which part of an activity is carried out through a formal agreement between a grantee and one or more other organizations.

Surrender
A grantee may decide to withdraw from the agreement thereby not completing the project as was planned. In such a scenario, the grantee is surrendering the project.
