MGMS - Roadmap

Multisite GMS provides for day to day project management. Moreover, because the system is web-based, can be assessed from anywhere, provides a medium for a collaborative working environment for internal/external experts and reviewers.

In today’s business environment, Grant-Giving Organizations need to be ever more accountable to its officials and Government Departments and indeed those who are accepted and rejected for various grants. MGMS can track each and every application there by making accountability, reporting and governance much easier. The system can also be easily integrated in future with leading financial systems to better keep track of spending.

The complete modules list for the MGMS is given below:

 

In progress

Review & rating of proposals

Workflow tool for Tracking & Monitoring  the award process

Publicize Notification of Project /  Grants by e-mail

Grant closure process

 

Document Management To have a complete record of all dealings and correspondence relating to applicants and applications .

To track all documents associated with the grant process that includes application,  reviews, correspondence, e-mails, mailing lists, uploaded documents, etc.

Will explore ways to integrate powerful document management features --Version control and document history

 

Digital Signature This technology is used to provide  non-repudiation  of data, authentication and data integrity services. By digitally signing the document, the person who signs it assures that he is the author of the document or the message that was signed.

To be used in Utilization certificate, Sanction order 

 

Relationship Management Support both email and online alert between grantor, grantee.

Configurable alerts between grantor, grantee and partner institutes when grant awarded, money spent, etc.

Track interactions between grantor, grantee.

Track communications sent to a grantee
