MGMS

Multisite Grant Management System, a module of Sakshat ERP has been designed to meet the unique needs within higher education and research institutes, including the specific needs of individual departments, centers, schools, colleges, and universities who receives Grants from Government / Non-Governmental Organizations and fruitfully utilizes to complete a project for which the grant is allocated.

It is an enterprise-wide system specifically designed for the needs of Funding and Grant making organizations. It controls all aspects of the funding process – from initial request, through assessment and recommendations, to authorization, payment and subsequent monitoring of outcomes.

MGMS also helps with accountability to MHRD, Govt. of India and provides additional online information regarding funds appropriated, allocated; the number of projects awarded; with projects begun and projects completed; and the number of jobs created or sustained by funds.

Designed and developed by Amrita Vishwa Vidya Peetham, MGMS is highly configurable and this flexibility supports the modern funding organization’s need to adapt and change.

 

How an organization is benefited through MGMS?

The accounting module within our grant management system include accounting functions for the management of hard money, clinical income, state funds, central funds, self-fund and others. Our grant management system is designed to supplement your general ledger or ERP system. The grant management system can be installed with an electronic interface to produce an integrated component or as an independently operated Standalone system. In either case, the result is a powerful grant management system designed for department, center, research, and grant administrators as well as principal investigators.

Our grant management system may be installed on an individual department or to serve an entire organization, depending on your financial accounting needs, it allows cost accounting codes to be designed for individual departments, centers, or faculty. MGMS includes a multi-tiered security system which gives different levels of authorizations to Users, Site Admins and Principal Investigators

 

Target Audience

It is especially suitable for funding organizations which:

* Have multiple funding streams
* Intend to provide web access to applicants, assessors and staff.
* Require a central contact database and CRM capability
* Need flexibility to meet funding demands
* Monitor outcomes as well as outputs
* Budgeting and
* Reports

 

Features

  • Interactive Project Dashboard.
  • Multi-level user management
  • MIS Reports and Statistical Reports
  • Keeps track of the Project Details such as Project name, code, Duration and Project Status.
  • Head of Accounts management at various levels.
  • Tracking grant period details on various projects.
  • Fund Allocation for various Projects.
  • Outsourcing of Sub Projects as well as management of Subprojects within the Organization.
