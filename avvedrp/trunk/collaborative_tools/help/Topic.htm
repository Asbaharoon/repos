Add/Edit Topic

 

 

This page is also accessible by clicking on the corresponding subject. Here, you can add, edit/ delete topics under the selected subject.

 

 

a)  Add new topic:

 

1.  Click on the link "Add New Topic”.

2. A small space will appear where you can add your topic name and description comes under the selected subject.

3. Click on the "Add" button.

 

 

 

b) Edit Topic:

 

1. You can click on the “Edit” icon given along with the respective topic.

2. A small window  will appear where you can enter your changes.

3. Click on the "Save" button.

 

 

c)  Delete Topic:

 

1. You can click on the “Delete” icon given along with the respective topic.

2. A pop-up window will appear to confirm the operation.

3.Click "OK" to continue.

 

 

 

[Important: All the above mentioned functionalities can only  be performed, if the access provided by the administrator.]
