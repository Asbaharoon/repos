How to Login?

   The user can login into the "Amrita Virtual Lab" by clicking on the "Log In" button provided in the home page.

    

 

Once you click the button, a pop window appears that allows you to sign in using any one of the accounts such as Google, Yahoo, Face book, my space, OpenID, twitter.

(Only Google is accessible now, rest of the accounts will be updated soon.)

 

[ Important : We do not store any user password.]
