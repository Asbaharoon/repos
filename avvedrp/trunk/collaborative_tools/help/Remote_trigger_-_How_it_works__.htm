Remote trigger - How it works?

The scheduler helps you to schedule the remote trigger device to your desired time if the its busy.

 

1. Click on the simulator of the particular remote trigger experiment.
 

2. If the device is busy, a message will display, "Sorry, the equipment is currently busy you can schedule the equipment for a later time.”    along with an “Experiment Scheduler”.
 

3.  Enter the details such as Start time, Experiment Duration and Email in the space provided to reschedule.
 

4. Click on the “Submit” button.
 

5. An automated email will send it your given email id. Click on the link given in the email to access the simulator in the scheduled time.
