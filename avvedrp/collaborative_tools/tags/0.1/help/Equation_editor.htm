Equation editor :

      This feature in the text editor provided in the "Edit Experiment Details" page will help you to insert or edit  equations in an experiment.

 

Steps:

    1. Click on the icon "ƒx" in the text editor.

    2. A pop-up window will appear where you can insert/edit equation.

    3. Click on the given functions to create/edit your equation.

     

    4. Click on "Copy to Document" button to copy to the text pad of the particular experiment.
